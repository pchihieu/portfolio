BINH PHUOC TRIP 2022

Written by: Phan Chí Hiếu

Purpose of the trip:To host a small playground for students from Dang Ha elementary and 3 others elemetary schools nearby.And also help these children to have a wonderful Tet holiday.

For the charity trip,I participated in 3 activities of the group,which consist:selling chicken floss to raise funds, as all the profit will be contributed to the later ones;setting up our own stalls where chilren can participate in to get gift prizes;For the last,we host a little Tet "concert" for the children and give prizes to the excellent students with difficulties.(PS:we also cook some tradition Vietnamese food for the occasion but unfortunately it can not be use for the activities)

The journey was time-consuming and laborous,but it was worth it.To see,understand the hardness that children here are facing and participating even a little to help the on the journey to overcome the difficulties to go to school did bring me a lot of joy.

This might be the insperation for me to do more meaningful work to help children with difficulties.
